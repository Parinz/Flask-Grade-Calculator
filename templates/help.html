FAQ

What are Core and Elective Subjects?


Core Subjects are subjects that are important as the name suggests these are normally:

  • Math/Algebra or a similar class
  • L.A or a similar class
  • Integrated/Science or a similar class
  • Geography/Ancient History or a similar class

There should only be 4 core subjects!

The rest of the subjects are electives.

There should be 10 Subjects in TOTAL


Do I have to put in the scores in a specific order?

No, however, you do need to classify between CORES AND ELECTIVES.
